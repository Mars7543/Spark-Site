About SPARK

SPARK'S Mission

Spark (Sharing Perspectives Among Real Kids) is a student-run leadership organization whose main goal is to foster a supportive environment for all members of the BCA community. SPARK acts as a resource to students, staff, parents, and the greater community. The central values of SPARK include service, personal growth, and respect.
